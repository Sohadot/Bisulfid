# Acquisition Disclaimer

status: draft

## Purpose

This disclaimer is intended for use on the bisulfid.com `/acquire/` page. When published, it will communicate that the acquisition page is a strategic inquiry surface for qualified buyers only, that bisulfid.com does not publicly name or target specific companies as acquirers, and that all acquisition inquiries are handled directly through the portfolio contact.

This disclaimer is required on the `/acquire/` route and is a condition of Quality Gate 10.

## Warning

This file is a draft. It is not yet attached to any published page or route.

Do not include this disclaimer in any public-facing content until it has been reviewed, approved, and linked to a published route in `main/data/routes.json`.

The acquisition page must not name specific company targets under any circumstances.
