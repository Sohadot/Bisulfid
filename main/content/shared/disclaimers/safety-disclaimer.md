# Safety Disclaimer

status: draft

## Purpose

This disclaimer is intended for use on bisulfid.com safety context pages (Layer 04 routes: `/sulfur-safety-context/`, `/hydrogen-sulfide-risk/`, `/sds-and-sulfur-terms/`, and any page touching high-risk terms). When published, it will communicate that bisulfid.com does not publish prescriptive safety advice, handling instructions, dosage guidance, or emergency response procedures. Readers will be directed to official Safety Data Sheets and qualified safety professionals.

This disclaimer is required on all Layer 04 safety governance pages before they may pass the Quality Gate.

## Warning

This file is a draft. It is not yet attached to any published page or route.

Do not include this disclaimer in any public-facing content until it has been reviewed, approved, and linked to a published route in `main/data/routes.json`.

No safety page may publish without this disclaimer in place.
