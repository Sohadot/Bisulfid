The glossary separates layers that must not be collapsed during **draft review**:

- **Lexical forms** - language-surface spellings and dictionary-style labels (for example German **-id** vs English **-ide** in cognate pairs) [SOURCE REQUIRED].
- **Chemical forms** - systematic or operational chemical naming intentions behind a label [SOURCE REQUIRED].
- **Candidate source support** - illustrative or teaching-backed hints usable only as **candidate support** until locked.
- **Approved claims** - **none** on this page; claim registries remain **inactive**, and no terminology claim is treated as approved here.
- **Terms requiring stronger source-locking** - any row in the controlled table marked for registry-grade work before public wording [SOURCE REQUIRED].
- **Terms that must not be confused** - explicit boundary notes (for example **bisulfite** vs **bisulfide** / sulfur anion vocabulary) kept in the table **not-to-confuse** column.
