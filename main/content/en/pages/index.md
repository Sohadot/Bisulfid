# bisulfid.com

**Global Sovereign Sulfur Intelligence Gateway**

bisulfid.com opens as a public sulfur gateway, but compounds into a strategic industrial reference asset.

---

## The Missing Letter

Bisulfide loses its final E at the German spelling boundary. Bisulfid — without the E — is not a typo. It is an architectural decision.

The domain holds the German-form spelling of the compound. That boundary is where this asset begins.

---

## What This Asset Is

bisulfid.com is a governed reference asset for sulfur compound terminology, nomenclature, source discipline, and multilingual intelligence.

It is not a chemistry textbook. It is not a commodity marketplace. It is a sovereign reference surface built around the spelling boundary between two languages and the industrial relevance of the compound at that boundary.
