## 1. Purpose: disambiguation authority draft

This page carries the **disambiguation** burden for three frequently conflated English labels:

- **Bisulfide** (and related salt naming)
- **Hydrosulfide** (often tied to **hydrogen sulfide** solution contexts in language, not handling advice here)
- **Sulfide** (class / ion naming in multiple contexts)

It is **not** a lab manual, SDS substitute, or ion equilibrium tutorial. It exists so bisulfid.com can **separate vocabulary roles** before any public copy asserts chemical facts.
