Spelling, suffix choice, and ion naming can diverge across languages and contexts (teaching slides, industrial SDS language, IUPAC systematic names, common names). **Draft review** assumption only: apparent parallels (for example **-id** vs **-ide**) need case-by-case verification [SOURCE REQUIRED].
