## What a Buyer Acquires

A full acquisition of bisulfid.com includes:

**Domain**
- bisulfid.com — the German-form spelling of a chemically and industrially relevant compound.

**Reference Framework**
- The governing doctrine layer (`doctrine/`) defining what this asset is, how it operates, and what quality conditions govern publication.

**Content Architecture**
- The source-layer English content drafts and planned multilingual content structure across seven languages.
- Multilingual directory structure with governed roles for EN, DE, AR, ZH, JA, FR, and ES layers.

**Terminology Ontology**
- The governed ontology skeleton: 14 planned sulfur compound terms, 11 spatial nodes for the Interactive Term Map, 12 semantic edges.
- The center-node position of bisulfid within the terminology system.

**Multilingual Architecture**
- Seven-language layer system with distinct strategic roles:
  - English: source layer
  - German: identity layer (nomenclature origin)
  - Arabic: market-chain layer
  - Chinese, Japanese, French, Spanish: extended coverage layers

**Source and Claim Governance**
- The source registry structure and claim registry framework — the machinery for ensuring that no claim reaches a public page without a traceable source.

**Future SEO Surface**
- 21 planned English routes, all in `planned` state, representing a future organic visibility surface for sulfur compound terminology, nomenclature, and industrial reference. None are indexed yet; the surface remains capturable.

**Future Newsletter Layer**
- A planned newsletter layer for sulfur intelligence is reserved in the route architecture but not yet built.

**Future Supplier Visibility Layer**
- A planned supplier visibility layer is reserved in the route architecture but not yet built.
